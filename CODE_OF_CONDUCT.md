# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, orientation, ability, or background.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that helps keep our community positive:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of different viewpoints  
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback  
- Showing empathy and kindness to others  

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, or personal attacks of any kind.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting a project maintainer.  
All complaints will be reviewed fairly and confidentially.
